What is the 12-Team Playoff?

How Teams Are Selected

  1. Conference Champions are placed automatically
  2. Highest-ranked teams fill remaining at-large spots
  3. Committee evaluates strength of schedule
  4. First round byes assigned to top four
  5. Bracket is finalized and seeded
  • Stregnth of Schedule
    • Top 10 Teams Played
    • Performance in more difficult games
  • Head-to-head results
  • Injuries to key players
  • Performance in last 4-6 weeks
  • Common opponents
